Introduction



An almanac created by Yu Li for Shigeo Fukuda research, 2025.

A project of Master of Design and Production, the University of Melbourne.

This research focuses on one key Japanese designer, Shigeo Fukuda,
who is a sculptor, graphic artist, and poster designer interested in optical illusions. He was active mainly between 1956 and 2009. His activities as a poster designer are highlighted in it.

This research explores and analyses the possible elements that affected his design. It also expands to broader areas such as modernism, visual perception, and semiotics.

In 'Annotated Bibliography', ten texts are listed with a brief introduction and analysis related to the topic.

In 'Research into Design', three terms related to the bibliography are introduced, with five images provided for each to further explain them.
