Google, Noto Color Emoji, 2015.



This image presents a set of emojis that are used in internet communication. ‘Emoji’ means ‘picture letter’ in Japanese.1 In emojis, human faces are abstracted to be a circle with eyes and a mouth on it. These emojis can be used to express different emotions through visual expressions that are also widely used in animation and comics, such as using hearteyes to express falling in love. It shows the use of the pictograph to convey human emotions on the internet, where people can not communicate face-to-face.

  1. Gunther Kress and Theo Van Leeuwen, Reading Images : The Grammar of Visual Design (Taylor & Francis Group, 2020), 30.
