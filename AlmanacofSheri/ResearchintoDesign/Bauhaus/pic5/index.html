OJoost Schmidt, Bauhaus 1923 Exhibition Poster, 1923.



Joost Schmidt studied and worked in the Bauhaus from 1919. He designed the poster for the Bauhaus Exhibition in 1923, shown in this image. The poster intended to advertise the avant-garde image of the Bauhaus and also its goals, focused on the balance of form and function.1 The Bauhaus logo, designed by Oskar Schlemmer, was subtly incorporated into the school title design of the poster while keeping the overall design consistent. The round and square motifs also respond to the elements used in the logo design.

  1. Magdalena Droste, The Bauhaus: 1919-1933 (Taschen, Berlin, 2019), 228-236.
