The Geography of Thought: How Westerners and Asians Think Differently And Why

Richard Nisbett, 2003

California: Free Press

In this book, 'Asians' mainly means Chinese, Japanese, and Korean people, and 'Westerners' typically refers to Americans but can also be extended to other Anglosphere countries. From the perspective of cultural psychology, Nisbett documents his international research and points out the differences in thinking between Westerners and Asians due to different ecologies, social structures, philosophies, and educational systems.
