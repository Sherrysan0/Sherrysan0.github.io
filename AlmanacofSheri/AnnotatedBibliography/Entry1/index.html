Fukuda Shigeo Shejicaiyouji
[Shigeo Fukuda Design]

Shigeo Fukuda, 2015

Translated by Yingyu Zhang

Taiwan: Pan Zhu Creative

This biography created by Shigeo Fukuda collects some comments from other people about him, as well as his own explorations, reflections, and opinions on the personal design life and the field of design.

FHe states that design exists to make life joyful. The book emphasises that, as a designer, he insists on observing and exploring even the most ordinary daily life around people with 'a heart for playing'. He not only pursues the maximisation of creative possibilities but also tries to challenge the established patterns and concepts with his unique ideas. Using techniques such as positive and negative shapes, contradiction space, and optical illusions, his works try to capture the most original vision of human beings and to resonate with the general public while beyond the usual vision, bringing the audience an incredible visual illusionist experience. He also draws courage and strength from his design life to insist and express himself, creating 'interesting' design works.
